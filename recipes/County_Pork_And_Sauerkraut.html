Slow Cooker Lancaster County Pork and Sauerkraut

Description

Ingredients

  • 1 (4 pound) pork loin roast
  • 1 teaspoon caraway seeds
  • 2 cups sauerkraut with liquid
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Cut pork loin, if necessary, to fit in the slow cooker.
  2. Season with caraway seeds, and salt and pepper to taste.
  3. Pour sauerkraut over the roast
  4. Cook on High for 1 hour, then cook on Low for 5 to 6 hours. Internal temperature of the roast should be at least 145 degrees Fahrenheit.
